Kathakali

Kathakali is a classical dance-drama form that originated in Kerala, known for its vibrant costumes, elaborate makeup, and expressive gestures. It tells stories from Indian epics like the Mahabharata and Ramayana, blending dance, music, and acting into a mesmerizing performance. The dancers, adorned with intricate face paint and headgear, convey emotions through precise facial expressions and hand gestures, making Kathakali a captivating cultural experience.
